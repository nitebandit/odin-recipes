Beef Bolognese

Description

Beef and mushroom bolognese starts with beef, mushrooms, fennel, and ground red pepper simmered with diced tomatoes before being tossed with pasta. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine
  • Olive Oil
  • Sirloin Beef
  • Yellow Onion
  • Salt
  • Ground Red Pepper
  • Finely Chopped Garlic
  • Fennel
  • Sliced Mushrooms
  • Tomato Paste
  • Diced Tomatoes

Recipe Steps

  1. Cook pasta.
  2. Add beef, onion, salt, pepper, garlic in skillet.
  3. Add fennel and mushrooms.
  4. Stir cooked pasta into skillet and simmer.
